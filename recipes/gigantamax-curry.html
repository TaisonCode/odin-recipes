Gigantamax Curry

Your Rhydon is out of control? Can't get your Blastoise to listen to you?

Get your pokemon tame and fully focused with this super effective recipe

Let's raise those stats and reach your final evolution!

RECPIPE

  • Water
  • Shuca Berry
  • Passho Berry
  • Yache Berry
  • Wacan Berry

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mash al the berries together with some (preferably with strenght or karate chops)
  2. Throw the mashed berries into a pot and add water (Water gun can be really helpfull here)
  3. Fan the flames! The goal here is to get to the biggest size flame, as quickly as possible, without burning it
  4. Put your heart into it! a little charm always does the trick
  5. (optional) If you are having trouble and you own a link cable you can get help from a friend!
  6. Give it to your pokemon team and get ready to face the elite four!
